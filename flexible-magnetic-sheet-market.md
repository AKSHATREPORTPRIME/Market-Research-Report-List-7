<p><p>The flexible magnetic sheet market is categorized into two main types: self-adhesive and no stickiness. Self-adhesive sheets feature an adhesive backing, allowing easy attachment to various surfaces, making them ideal for crafting, signage, and promotional materials. On the other hand, no stickiness sheets are designed for applications where adhesive is unnecessary or undesirable, enabling users to cut and reposition them without residue. Both types cater to diverse industrial, commercial, and personal needs, emphasizing practicality and versatility.</p></p>
