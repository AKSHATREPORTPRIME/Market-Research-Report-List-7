<p><strong>The Escalator Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Chains</li><li>Drive Chains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Escalator chain markets can be categorized into two main types: step chains and drive chains. Step chains are designed specifically for escalators, providing stable support for the steps as they move along the escalator's incline. Drive chains, on the other hand, facilitate the movement of the entire escalator system, connecting the motor to the escalator steps. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safe and efficient operation of escalators, with their specific designs catering to distinct functional requirements within the system.</p></p>
