<p><p>Hydrotherapy tanks are categorized into two main types: full body entry and partial body entry. Full body entry tanks are designed for complete immersion, allowing users to experience therapeutic benefits over the entire body, making them ideal for conditions requiring extensive treatment. In contrast, partial body entry tanks focus on specific body regions, enabling targeted therapy for ailments like joint pain or injuries. Both types enhance relaxation and promote healing through controlled temperature and buoyancy, catering to diverse therapeutic needs.</p></p>
