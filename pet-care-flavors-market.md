<p><ul><li>Water Soluble Flavors</li><li>Oil Soluble Flavors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pet care flavors market is divided primarily into water-soluble and oil-soluble flavors. Water-soluble flavors are typically used in products that require easy mixing with liquid formulations, such as pet food additives and supplements. These flavors ensure uniform distribution and quick absorption. Conversely, oil-soluble flavors are ideal for fatty formulations, such as treats and kibble, enhancing taste while improving product stability. Each type caters to specific product formulations, influencing the sensory experience and overall palatability for pets.</p></p>
