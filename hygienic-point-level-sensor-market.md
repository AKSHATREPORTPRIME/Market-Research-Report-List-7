<p><p>The Hygienic Point Level Sensor market is divided into two main types: contact type and non-contact type sensors. Contact type sensors directly interact with the fluid or solid material to provide accurate level measurement, making them suitable for industries like food and beverage where hygiene is critical. Non-contact type sensors, on the other hand, utilize methods like radar or ultrasonic waves to detect levels without physical contact, offering benefits in terms of cleanliness and reduced risk of contamination in sensitive applications.</p></p>
