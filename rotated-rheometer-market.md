<p><ul><li>Stress Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li><li>Strain Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotated rheometer market is categorized into two primary types: stress-controlled and strain-controlled rheometers. Stress-controlled rheometers measure the flow and deformation of materials under controlled stress, making them suitable for applications where precise stress conditions are required. In contrast, strain-controlled rheometers apply a specific strain to the material and measure the resulting stress, providing insights into the material's elastic and viscous properties. Each type serves distinct applications in industries such as food, cosmetics, and polymers, enabling comprehensive material characterization.</p></p>
