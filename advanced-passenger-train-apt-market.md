The APT-E (Experimental) emphasized innovative technology, featuring tilting mechanics for enhanced stability and speed on existing track systems. In contrast, the APT-P (Prototype) focused on proving the operational capabilities of these advancements in real-world scenarios. Both market types aimed to revolutionize rail travel through improved efficiency, comfort, and speed, ultimately influencing future train designs.</p></p>
