<p><p>Remote control (RC) helicopters are categorized into three main market types based on their control channels. Three-channel helicopters are basic models, typically controlling throttle, yaw, and pitch, making them suitable for beginners. Four-channel helicopters add roll control, allowing for more advanced maneuvers and stability. Six-channel helicopters offer even greater versatility, incorporating advanced features like 3D capabilities and greater agility, catering to experienced pilots seeking complex flight dynamics. Each type targets different skill levels and flying preferences in the RC market.</p></p>
