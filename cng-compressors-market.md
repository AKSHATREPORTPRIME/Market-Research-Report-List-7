<p><p>The CNG compressors market consists of two primary types: rotary and reciprocating compressors. Rotary compressors utilize rotating elements to compress the gas, offering continuous flow and compact design, making them suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, reciprocating compressors employ pistons to compress gas in cycles, providing high pressure and efficiency, ideal for larger-scale operations. Each type serves distinct needs within the market, influenced by factors such as application requirements, efficiency, and space constraints.</p></p>
