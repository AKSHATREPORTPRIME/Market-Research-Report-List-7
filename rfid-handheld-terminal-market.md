<p><p>The RFID handheld terminal market is categorized into several system types based on frequency ranges. LF RFID systems operate at low frequencies, typically used for applications like animal tracking and access control. HF RFID systems function at higher frequencies and are commonly employed in libraries and payment systems. UHF RFID systems cover ultra-high frequencies, ideal for inventory management and supply chain logistics due to their longer read ranges. Other markets include active and semi-passive RFID systems, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
