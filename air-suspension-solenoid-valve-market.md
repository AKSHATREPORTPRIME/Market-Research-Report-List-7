<p><ul><li>Three Ports</li><li>Six Ports</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air suspension solenoid valve market comprises two primary types: three-port and six-port valves. Three-port valves manage air flow to one air spring, controlling ride height and comfort. They typically feature a single inlet and two outlets for air distribution. In contrast, six-port valves serve multiple air springs, allowing for more sophisticated control across multiple vehicle axes. This versatility ensures better stability and performance, making them popular in advanced air suspension systems in both commercial and luxury vehicles.</p></p>
