<p><p>CTC detection markets are categorized into three main types. Immunoaffinity enrichment utilizes antibodies to capture circulating tumor cells, enhancing sensitivity and specificity. Physical property enrichment focuses on separating CTCs based on their distinct size, density, or electrophoretic mobility, offering a more universal approach. Other market methods may include microfluidics or cell-culture techniques that do not fit neatly into the previous categories. Together, these methodologies advance cancer diagnostics and monitoring by effectively isolating and analyzing CTCs from blood samples.</p></p>
