<p><p>The Automotive Brushed Motor is a type of electric motor widely used in various vehicle applications, including power windows, wipers, and HVAC systems. Characterized by their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, brushed motors operate using brushes that make contact with rotating commutators to facilitate power delivery. This technology, while facing competition from brushless motors, continues to find relevance in the automotive industry due to its established reliability and performance in low to moderate power applications.</p><p>The Automotive Brushed Motor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period. Manufacturers are focusing on innovations that reduce weight, improve thermal performance, and extend the lifespan of brushed motors.
