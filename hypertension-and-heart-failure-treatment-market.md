<p><p>The hypertension and heart failure treatment market comprises various drug classes. Diuretics help reduce fluid volume to lower blood pressure and relieve fluid overload in heart failure. Beta blockers decrease heart rate and workload on the heart. ACE inhibitors relax blood vessels, improving blood flow and decreasing blood pressure. Alpha blockers also relax blood vessels but work differently, targeting nerve impulses. Other medications, including calcium channel blockers and vasodilators, provide additional treatment options to manage these conditions effectively.</p></p>
