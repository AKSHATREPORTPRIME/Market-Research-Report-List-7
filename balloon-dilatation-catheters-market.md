<p><p>Balloon Dilatation Catheters are specialized medical devices used to widen narrowed or obstructed blood vessels or ducts, primarily in procedures involving angioplasty. These devices are essential in treating conditions such as coronary artery disease and peripheral artery disease.</p><p>The Balloon Dilatation Catheters Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in catheter technology, and a growing aging population.
