<p><ul><li>Plain Weave</li><li>Leno Weave</li><li>Twill Weave</li><li>Satin Weave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Glass Fabrics market consists of various weaving types, each serving distinct applications. Plain weave offers high strength and stability, making it suitable for insulation and reinforcement. Leno weave incorporates crossed warp threads, providing superior dimensional stability and is ideal for applications requiring lightweight and flexible materials. Twill weave features a diagonal pattern, enhancing drape and durability, while satin weave provides a smooth surface with high aesthetic appeal, often used in high-end electronics and automotive components.</p></p>
