<p><p>The video door phone market can be categorized into two main types: WiFi and DECT. WiFi-enabled video door phones utilize internet connectivity to transmit video and audio signals, allowing users to interact remotely via smartphones or tablets. In contrast, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) video door phones focus on direct wireless communication, ensuring reliability and a secure connection without relying on internet bandwidth. Both types enhance home security, providing visual identification of visitors and facilitating two-way conversations.</p></p>
