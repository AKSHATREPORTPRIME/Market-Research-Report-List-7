<p><strong>The Automatic Checkweigher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic checkweigher market is primarily divided into two types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously measure the weight of products moving on a conveyor belt, ensuring real-time monitoring and high-speed inspection, ideal for high-volume production lines. In contrast, intermittent checkweighers weigh products at fixed intervals, pausing the line momentarily for accurate measurements, making them suitable for applications requiring precise weight determination. Both types enhance product quality control and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
