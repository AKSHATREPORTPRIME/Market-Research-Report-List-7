<p><ul><li>BTT Therapy</li><li>BTD Therapy</li><li>Destination Therapy</li><li>BTR Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LVAD market is segmented into four primary therapy types. BTT (Bridge to Transplant) therapy supports patients awaiting heart transplants. BTD (Bridge to Decision) therapy is for those needing further evaluation before transplant eligibility. Destination Therapy serves patients with no transplant options, providing long-term support. BTR (Bridge to Recovery) therapy is aimed at patients who may regain sufficient heart function. Each therapy offers tailored solutions to address different stages and needs in advanced heart failure management.</p></p>
