PEMFC forklifts are widely used due to their high efficiency and quick refueling capabilities, making them ideal for warehouse applications. DMFC forklifts utilize methanol as fuel, offering advantages in storage and handling. Other types may include alkaline and solid oxide fuel cells, each with unique benefits tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
