<p><p>Lead foil tape is categorized into two main types based on adhesive material: acrylic and rubber. Acrylic lead foil tape offers excellent temperature resistance and UV stability, making it suitable for outdoor applications and environments with fluctuating temperatures. In contrast, rubber lead foil tape provides superior adhesion on various surfaces and is often preferred for indoor uses where flexibility and easy repositioning are required. Both types serve essential roles in electrical shielding, soundproofing, and protection against moisture.</p></p>
