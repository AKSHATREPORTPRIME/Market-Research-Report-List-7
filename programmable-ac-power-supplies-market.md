<p><p>The programmable AC power supplies market primarily features two types: Linear AC Power Supplies and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) AC Power Supplies. Linear AC power supplies deliver a stable and low-noise output by utilizing transformers and linear regulators, ideal for precision applications. In contrast, PWM AC power supplies use rapid switching techniques to control output voltage and frequency, offering improved efficiency and compact design, making them suitable for a variety of industrial and laboratory applications. Both types cater to specific power requirements.</p></p>
