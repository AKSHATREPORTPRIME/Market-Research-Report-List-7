<p><p>The injectable bone graft market is primarily divided into two types: natural and synthetic. Natural bone grafts, derived from human or animal tissues, promote osteogenic activity and optimal healing due to their biocompatibility. Synthetic bone grafts, made from materials like calcium phosphate or bioactive glass, offer a customizable solution with reduced risk of disease transmission. Both types are used in orthopedic, dental, and spinal surgeries to enhance bone regeneration, each with unique advantages depending on clinical needs.</p></p>
