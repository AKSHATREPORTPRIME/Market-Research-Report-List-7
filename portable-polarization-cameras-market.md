<p><p>The Portable Polarization Cameras market is segmented into two main types: Fully Polarized Light and Partially Polarized Light. Fully Polarized Light cameras capture images wherein the light's electric field oscillates in a single direction, providing high contrast and clarity, ideal for applications like industrial inspections and scientific research. In contrast, Partially Polarized Light cameras capture light that has varying degrees of polarization, offering versatility for environments with mixed lighting conditions, making them suitable for wildlife observation and outdoor photography.</p></p>
