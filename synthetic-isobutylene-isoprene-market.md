Regular IIR is known for its excellent impermeability to gases and resistance to temperature, making it suitable for various applications like automotive and industrial seals. Halogenated IIR, modified through the addition of halogens, offers enhanced chemical resistance and improved curing properties, ideal for applications requiring higher durability.
