<p><p>Sonar systems are categorized into several types based on their scanning capabilities. Single Beam Scanning Sonar Systems emit one sonar beam to measure water depth and detect objects directly beneath a vessel, ideal for simple surveys. Multi-beam Sonar Systems utilize multiple beams to create detailed 3D maps of the seafloor, enhancing data richness and accuracy. Side Scan Sonar Systems capture images of the seafloor by emitting sonar waves at an angle, making them effective for locating submerged objects. Other market types include specialized systems for unique applications.</p></p>
