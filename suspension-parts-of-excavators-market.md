<p><ul><li>Rigid Connected Type</li><li>Semi-Floating</li><li>Full Floating</li><li>Independent Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suspension parts of excavators play a crucial role in maintaining stability and comfort during operation. In the rigid connected type, the suspension is fixed, offering limited flexibility. Semi-floatings provide some mobility, enhancing performance on uneven terrain. Full floating systems allow for maximum articulation, improving operator comfort and equipment control. Independent mounted suspensions offer individual wheel movement, enhancing maneuverability and traction. Each type is designed to optimize performance based on the specific demands of various excavation tasks.</p></p>
