<p><p>Aluminum Composite Material (ACM) panels have distinct market types, primarily categorized into PVDF Coated ACP and PE Coated ACP. PVDF Coated ACP features a fluoropolymer resin coating that ensures superior weather resistance and durability, making it suitable for architectural applications in harsh environments. In contrast, PE Coated ACP utilizes a polyethylene core, offering a cost-effective solution with good aesthetic appeal, primarily used in indoor applications and lighter exteriors. Both types cater to diverse construction and design needs across various sectors.</p></p>
