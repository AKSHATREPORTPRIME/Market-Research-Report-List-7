<p><p>The daily wear contact lenses market includes three main types: soft contact lenses, hybrid contact lenses, and rigid contact lenses. Soft contact lenses, made from flexible materials, provide comfort and are widely used. Hybrid contact lenses combine a rigid center with a soft outer layer, offering the advantages of both types. Rigid gas-permeable lenses, known for their durability and sharp vision correction, are less commonly used but are suitable for specific vision needs. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in vision correction.</p></p>
