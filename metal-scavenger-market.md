<p><p>The metal scavenger market is divided into two primary types: acidic and alkaline chemical scavengers. Acidic chemical scavengers are designed to capture and neutralize metal ions in environments with low pH levels, making them effective in various industrial processes such as oil refining and chemical manufacturing. Conversely, alkaline chemical scavengers function in higher pH conditions, targeting metal ions common in processes like wastewater treatment and metal extraction. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing product purity and managing metal contaminants.</p></p>
