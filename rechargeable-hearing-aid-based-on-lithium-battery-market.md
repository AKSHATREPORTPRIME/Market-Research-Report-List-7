<p><p>The rechargeable hearing aid market is segmented into four main types: Receiver-in-Canal (RIC), Behind-the-Ear (BTE), In-the-Ear (ITE), and In-the-Canal (ITC). RIC hearing aids offer discreet designs with external receivers for improved sound quality. BTE models sit behind the ear and are suitable for severe hearing loss. ITE aids fit directly in the ear, providing comfort and aesthetics, while ITC devices are smaller and less visible. All types leverage lithium batteries for convenience and sustainability.</p></p>
