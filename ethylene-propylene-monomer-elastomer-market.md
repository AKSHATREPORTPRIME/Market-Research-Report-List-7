<p><p>The Ethylene Propylene Monomer Elastomer market encompasses two primary types of polymerization processes: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent, enabling better control over molecular weight and properties, resulting in flexible and high-performance elastomers. In contrast, suspension polymerization uses water as a medium, leading to a higher polymer yield and improved particle size distribution. Both methods contribute to the production of versatile elastomers utilized in various applications, including automotive, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
