<p><p>The Manual Metal Arc (MMA) electrodes market consists of three primary types: cellulosic, rutile, and basic electrodes. Cellulosic electrodes contain cellulose and are known for their rapid welding speed and deep penetration, making them ideal for vertical and overhead applications. Rutile electrodes offer a smooth arc and are suitable for general-purpose welding, providing good bead appearance. Basic electrodes are designed to reduce impurities and are used for welding high-strength steels, offering superior toughness and resistance to cracking.</p></p>
