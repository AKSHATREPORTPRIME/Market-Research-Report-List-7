<p><ul><li>Shear Static Mixer</li><li>Positive Displacement Mixer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fluid Mixer market comprises various types, including shear static mixers and positive displacement mixers. Shear static mixers utilize mechanical design to create turbulence, promoting efficient blending of fluids without moving parts, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent mixing. In contrast, positive displacement mixers use a mechanism to directly push fluid through the mixer, ensuring precise flow rates and substantial mixing power. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing process efficiency and product quality in fluid handling applications.</p></p>
