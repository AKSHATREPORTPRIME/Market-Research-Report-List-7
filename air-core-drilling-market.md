<p><strong>The Air Core Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dust Drilling</li><li>Mist Drilling</li><li>Foam Drilling</li><li>Aerated Fluid Drilling</li><li>Nitrogen Membrane Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air core drilling market encompasses various techniques for subsurface exploration. Dust drilling utilizes compressed air to lift cuttings, ideal for shallow formations. Mist drilling combines air and water for enhanced cooling and lubrication. Foam drilling employs foam as a drilling fluid to improve stability and reduce water usage. Aerated fluid drilling utilizes a mixture of air and fluid for effective cuttings transport. Nitrogen membrane drilling utilizes nitrogen to create a controlled environment, minimizing water interaction and enhancing efficiency in challenging conditions.</p></p>
