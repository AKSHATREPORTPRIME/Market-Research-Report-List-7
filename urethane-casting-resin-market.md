Rigid casting resin is known for its durability and strength, making it suitable for creating hard, structural components and prototypes. In contrast, soft casting resin offers flexibility and is ideal for applications requiring a softer touch, such as molds or cushioning parts. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, arts and crafts, and product design, fulfilling specific requirements in functionality and design.</p></p>
