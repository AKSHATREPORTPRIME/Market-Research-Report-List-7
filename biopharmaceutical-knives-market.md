<p><p>The biopharmaceutical knives market is primarily segmented into rotary slicing tools and slide slicing tools. Rotary slicing tools utilize a circular blade that rotates to cut through materials, offering precision and efficiency for tissue and cell samples. In contrast, slide slicing tools employ a straight blade that moves in a linear fashion, ideal for creating thin sections of biological specimens. Both types serve critical roles in laboratory settings, facilitating accurate analysis and processing of biopharmaceutical products and research materials.</p></p>
