<p><p>Sapphire Optical Lenses are categorized into two primary types: spherical and aspherical. Spherical lenses have a consistent curvature and are typically easier to manufacture, offering a uniform focus. They are commonly used in applications where simplicity is key. In contrast, aspherical lenses feature a complex profile, allowing for better control of light and reduced optical aberrations. This makes them ideal for high-precision applications such as cameras, lasers, and medical devices, where enhanced image clarity and performance are essential.</p></p>
