<p><ul><li>Storage Heating</li><li>Instantaneous Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart toilet seats market can be categorized into two main heating types: storage heating and instantaneous heating. Storage heating systems preheat water in a reservoir, ensuring a ready supply during use, making them efficient for multiple uses in quick succession. In contrast, instantaneous heating systems heat water on demand, providing a continuous flow without a storage tank, thus conserving energy and reducing water waste. Both types enhance user comfort and promote hygiene in modern bathrooms.</p></p>
