<p><p>Polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) binder is crucial for lithium-ion batteries, enhancing electrode stability and overall performance. Two primary market types for PVDF production are suspension polymerization and emulsion polymerization. Suspension polymerization involves dispersing monomers in a liquid medium, resulting in a granular product, suitable for scaling up. Emulsion polymerization, on the other hand, involves polymerizing monomers in water with surfactants, producing a fine latex, beneficial for high-performance applications. Both methods cater to specific industrial needs in the battery sector.</p></p>
