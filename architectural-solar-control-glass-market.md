<p><p>The Architectural Solar Control Glass market comprises three primary types: single glazed, double glazed, and triple glazed. Single glazed glass offers minimal thermal insulation and is primarily used in low-cost constructions. Double glazed units consist of two panes, enhancing insulation and reducing heat transfer, making them ideal for energy-efficient buildings. Triple glazed glass features three panes for superior insulation and solar control, suitable for extreme climates. Each type balances cost, energy efficiency, and performance, catering to diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
