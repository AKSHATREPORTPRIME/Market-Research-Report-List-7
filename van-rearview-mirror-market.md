<p><p>The van rearview mirror market encompasses three main types: exterior mirrors, interior mirrors, and under rearview mirrors. Exterior mirrors are mounted on the vehicle's sides, crucial for visibility and safety during driving maneuvers. Interior mirrors, located inside the vehicle, provide a wide view of the rear and help prevent blind spots. Under rearview mirrors, typically designed for specialized vehicles, enhance visibility below the rear window, aiding in parking and navigation in tight spaces, thereby improving overall driving safety.</p></p>
