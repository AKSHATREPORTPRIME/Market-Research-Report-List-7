<p><p>The three-phase string inverter market is categorized into two main types: on-grid and off-grid string inverters. On-grid string inverters are designed to connect solar systems to the utility grid, allowing users to sell excess energy back to the grid and reduce electricity costs. In contrast, off-grid string inverters are used in isolated systems, enabling solar energy generation without a connection to the grid, and typically include battery storage for energy use during non-sunny periods. Both types enhance energy efficiency and renewable adoption.</p></p>
