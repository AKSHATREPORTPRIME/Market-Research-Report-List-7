<p><p>Three-Way Catalysts (TWC) are essential components used in automotive exhaust systems to convert harmful pollutants from internal combustion engines into less harmful emissions. They facilitate three simultaneous chemical reactions: the oxidation of carbon monoxide to carbon dioxide, the oxidation of unburned hydrocarbons to carbon dioxide and water, and the reduction of nitrogen oxides to nitrogen and oxygen.
