<p><strong>The Preaction Sprinkler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non Interlock</li><li>Single Interlock</li><li>Double Interlock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Preaction sprinkler systems are crucial for fire protection, with three main types: Non-Interlock, Single Interlock, and Double Interlock. Non-Interlock systems activate sprinklers automatically upon fire detection, allowing rapid response. Single Interlock systems require both a fire detection signal and a manual valve to be opened before sprinklers activate, enhancing control. Double Interlock systems provide the highest security, requiring fire detection, manual valve operation, and pressurization, minimizing accidental discharges while ensuring effective protection against fires.</p></p>
