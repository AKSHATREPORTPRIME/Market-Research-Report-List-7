<p><p>The pneumatic atomizing nozzles market includes three main types: flat spray nozzles, full cone nozzles, and hollow cone nozzles. Flat spray nozzles produce a flat, fan-like spray pattern, ideal for coating applications. Full cone nozzles deliver a uniform spray in a circular pattern, suitable for cooling, washing, or spraying chemicals. Hollow cone nozzles create a hollow spray pattern, maximizing surface coverage for applications like fine misting or fog generation. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
