<p><ul><li>Complete Electrification</li><li>Partial Electrification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transportation electrification encompasses two primary market types: complete electrification and partial electrification. In complete electrification, all transportation modes, including vehicles, rail, and shipping, fully transition to electric power sources, eliminating reliance on fossil fuels. Conversely, partial electrification involves integrating electric technologies into existing systems, where only certain segments or vehicles, such as hybrid electric vehicles or electric buses, are utilized alongside traditional fuel-powered options. This approach allows for gradual implementation and adaptation to electric mobility, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
