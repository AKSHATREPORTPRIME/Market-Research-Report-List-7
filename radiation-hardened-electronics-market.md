<p><p>The Radiation Hardened Electronics market encompasses two primary types: Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD) and Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP). RHBD focuses on designing circuits and systems to withstand radiation effects, employing specialized techniques like error detection and redundancy. In contrast, RHBP involves modifying manufacturing processes and materials to enhance the inherent radiation resistance of semiconductor devices. Both approaches aim to ensure reliable performance in high-radiation environments like space, nuclear facilities, and high-energy physics applications.</p></p>
