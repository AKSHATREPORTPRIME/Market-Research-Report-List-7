<p><p>The Steam Methane Reforming (SMR) market is categorized into two main types based on downstream processing methods. Steam Methane Reforming with Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) involves the separation of hydrogen from other gases at high pressure, providing high-purity hydrogen. Conversely, Steam Methane Reforming with Amine Absorption utilizes chemical solvents to selectively absorb and remove carbon dioxide from syngas, enhancing hydrogen yield. Both methods are crucial for optimizing hydrogen production while minimizing environmental impact in various industrial applications.</p></p>
