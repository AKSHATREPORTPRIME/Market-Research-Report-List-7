<p><p>The sealed twin-screw pump market is categorized into Single Volute and Double Volute pumps. Single Volute Twin Screw Pumps feature a single chamber for fluid transport, offering compact design and is ideal for lower flow applications. In contrast, Double Volute Twin Screw Pumps have two interconnected volutes, providing enhanced flow handling and reduced pulsation, making them suitable for higher capacity and more demanding operations. Both types are utilized in various industries for their efficiency, reliability, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
